About CSGO Skins

CSGO offers players an insane amount of variety of CSGO Skins to select from. New cases are put in almost every 4 to 6 months. With these skyrocketing skins, CSGO Third party gambling sites have started to present players with many kinds of Casino games offering big rewards. But that's not it! In today's world, CSGO has become huge in professional sport/esport, you can just bet on the matches as if it was like soccer or basketball. This is way different from skin gambling, because in eSports you bet real money instead of skins. The idea behind CSGO casino games is essentially the same one as offered by standard gambling sites, the main difference being you're using a skin as a currency instead of chips.

How does CSGO Skin Gambling work?

In Counter-Strike: Global Offensive players can buy CSGO Skins from the Steam Market and they can also buy CSGO Cases so they can open up the Cases with bought Keys. When they open up the Cases with the Keys the player will receieve a Skin, the quality and price of the skins vary a lot. Selling the skins on the Steam Market is possible or you can trade in the Skins to betting sites to receive coins to gamble with. Although these skins have no direct influence on gameplay, they are very popular, the most rarest skins are sold for over tens of thousands of dollars on different Third-party market sites and the Steam Market. Skins are used to bet on the outcome of tournament matches and used as a currency to gamble on casino games like coinflip, crash, roulette, and jackpot and many more gambling games. Skin gambling is huge in the gaming industry, and it exists in a many video games, for example Dota 2, TF2 and many more.

The most popular Betting Games in CSGO.

Roulette

CSGO Roulette was one of the first betting games for Counter-Strike, now it's a classic that everyone loves to play. The rules are way more simple than the normal Roulette you see in Vegas, just put a bet on the 3 colors you see on the table and bet on what you think it will land on and then you wait and pray. Simple, addicting and a very fun betting game.

Crash

CSGO Crash is a high risk reward gambling game. You can win huge prizes but it's very risky. When you've placed a bet, a line will start going up and a multiplier will start rising. If you don't click the "stop button" and withdraw quick enough, the multiplier might stop and ’crash’ making you lose the bet you placed.

Coinflip

CSGO Coinflip is very simple to understand, the coin has two sides, In CSGO Coinflip you place bets against another player, the odds will change depending on the worth of your pot. An example, if you’ve placed a CSGO Skin worth $5 and another player has a skin worth $2, you may get better odds as you’ve got more to lose. This adds some extra excitement to the Coinflip gamemode for CSGO.

Jackpot

CSGO Jackpot requires two players or more deposit coins into the pot. Every cent you place, you get 1 ticket so the value of your skins is how many tickets you will be awarded. The value of the skins is determined by the Steam Analyst page. After you've joined a game of CSGO Jackpot, you get an estimated chance of winning based on how large a share of the total pot you stand for. If the total pot is 100 tickets and you have entered a total value of 50 tickets, you have a 50% chance of winning.
